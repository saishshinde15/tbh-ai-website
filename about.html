Leading the AI Security Revolution

tbh.ai is at the forefront of developing secure, trustworthy AI systems for enterprise and research applications. We combine cutting-edge research with practical implementation to create AI security solutions that address the complex challenges of today's rapidly evolving threat landscape.

Our team brings together expertise in machine learning, cybersecurity, and software engineering to create comprehensive security frameworks that protect AI systems at every level—from model architecture to deployment infrastructure.

With a growing portfolio of security-focused AI models on HuggingFace and open-source frameworks on GitHub, we're committed to advancing the field of AI security through innovation, collaboration, and education.

Our Mission

"Secure this AI revolution by embedding security, trust, and resilience into every layer of AI systems — from models to agents to applications."

At tbh.ai, we are on a mission to build secure, scalable, and trustworthy AI systems for a rapidly evolving world. In an age where AI adoption is accelerating, security can no longer be an afterthought — it must be foundational.

We aim to enable organizations and developers to build not just powerful AI, but safe, ethical, and reliable AI — ready for the real-world challenges ahead.

Our Methodology

A systematic approach to securing AI systems

1. Comprehensive Threat Modeling

We begin by identifying potential threats and vulnerabilities specific to AI systems, from prompt injections to model poisoning, creating a detailed threat landscape.

2. Layered Defense Architecture

Our security approach implements multiple layers of protection, ensuring that if one layer is compromised, others remain intact to maintain system integrity.

3. Continuous Security Validation

We employ rigorous testing and validation processes, including red-team exercises and penetration testing, to ensure our security measures remain effective against evolving threats.

4. Ongoing Monitoring & Adaptation

Our solutions include continuous monitoring capabilities that detect and respond to potential security incidents in real-time, with regular updates to address new threat vectors.
