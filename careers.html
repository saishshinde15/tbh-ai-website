Join Our Mission

At tbh.ai, we're building the future of secure AI systems. We're looking for talented, passionate individuals who share our vision of creating trustworthy AI that can be safely deployed in critical applications.

As a growing company at the intersection of AI and security, we offer unique opportunities to work on cutting-edge technology that addresses some of the most pressing challenges in the field.

Our Work Policy

At tbh.ai, we believe in creating a flexible, supportive work environment that enables our team members to do their best work while maintaining a healthy work-life balance. Our work policy is designed to foster innovation, collaboration, and personal growth.

Remote-First Approach

  • We operate as a primarily remote company, allowing team members to work from anywhere with a reliable internet connection.
  • Team members have the flexibility to create their optimal work environment and schedule, with core hours for collaboration.
  • We provide stipends for home office setup to ensure everyone has a comfortable and productive workspace.

Occasional In-Person Collaboration

  • We organize quarterly team gatherings for strategic planning, team building, and fostering personal connections.
  • Project kickoffs and major milestones may involve optional in-person collaboration sessions.
  • Travel expenses for company-organized gatherings are covered by tbh.ai.

Communication & Collaboration

  • We use asynchronous communication as our default, respecting different time zones and work schedules.
  • Regular video meetings keep teams aligned and foster a sense of community.
  • We document decisions and discussions thoroughly to maintain transparency and knowledge sharing.

Professional Development

  • We provide a dedicated budget for learning resources, conference attendance, and professional certifications.
  • Regular knowledge-sharing sessions allow team members to learn from each other's expertise.
  • Career growth paths are clearly defined, with regular feedback and mentorship opportunities.
